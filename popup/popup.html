Explain Complex Topics Simply

Explain the following concept in a clear and structured way as if you were teaching someone with no prior knowledge of the subject. Start with a simple definition, then break the concept into smaller parts. Use real-world examples and analogies whenever possible. Avoid unnecessary technical jargon and keep the explanation easy to understand while still being accurate.
